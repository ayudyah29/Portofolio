Sertificate

MTCNA

MikroTik certification program for entry-level network engineers. This certification helps Network Engineers understand the basics of networking, improve their skills, and add value to their careers.

JavaScrip

Formal recognition that shows that someone has knowledge and skills in the JavaScript programming language, both for front-end and back-end web development. This certification can increase one's credibility and career opportunities in the technology industry.

Fortinet

A training and certification program provided by Fortinet, a company that provides network security solutions. This certification aims to validate an individual's knowledge and skills in using and managing Fortinet products, as well as network security in general.

Red Had

To validate the knowledge and skills of IT professionals in managing and operating the Red Hat Enterprise Linux (RHEL) operating system. These certifications range from basic to advanced levels, such as RHCSA (Red Hat Certified System Administrator), RHCE (Red Hat Certified Engineer), and RHCA (Red Hat Certified Architect).

IDN Academy

A program from IDN Boarding School that invites students to teach and share IT knowledge to other schools. This program aims to improve the quality of education and expand the reach of IT knowledge that they have mastered.

MTCTCE

"MikroTik Certified Traffic Control Engineer", which is an advanced certification from MikroTik that focuses on managing and controlling traffic (traffic control) on the network. This certification proves a deep understanding of MikroTik features related to bandwidth management, firewalls, and network traffic settings.
